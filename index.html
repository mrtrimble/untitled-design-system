Design System

Colors

You can bring your own colors to this design system! Add colors to the ./tokens/colors.json file and Style Dictionery will create tokens out of them!

Once the tokens are created, running the build script will compile Sass and output a stylesheet that includes custom properties and color utility classes for each color.

Buttons

There are three different types of buttons: primary, secondary, and plain.

Primary buttons are for primary actions, secondary buttons are for secondary actions. Plain buttons are styled to appear as links.
